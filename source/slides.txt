		<slide label="p1-pretext-what-is-it">
			<title>Part I: What Is PreTeXt?</title>

			<p>
				PreTeXt is an open-source authoring system for writing structured, accessible, and interactive educational materials.
			</p>

			<dl pause="no">
				<li>
					<title>Write once, publish everywhere</title>
					<p>Web, PDF, ePub, large print, and Braille from a single source.</p>
				</li>
				<li>
					<title>Pedagogy-aware structure</title>
					<p>Examples, exercises, definitions, and loads of interactive features.</p>
				</li>
				<li>
					<title>Accessibility by design</title>
					<p>Screen-reader friendly, responsive layouts, and dark/light modes.</p>
				</li>
			</dl>

			<p>
				Whether you want an interactive textbook, an research hub, or lecture notes, PreTeXt is ready for most workflows.
			</p>
		</slide>
